The Best Movies According to Ben

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Howl's Moving Castle

My wife and I love this movie, visually it's stunning and the characters are just so loveable.

How to Train Your Dragon

Hiccup's ingenuity and willingness to test boundries makes him a great role model for children and adults.

The Dark Knight

Heath Ledgers performance as the Joker is beyond creepy and really makes you feel the insanity that is the character.
